About Our Company

Precision GIS and Technology Consultancy Ltd is a Ghana-based company delivering innovative GIS and IT systems for real-world transformation in energy, health, agriculture, environment, and urban planning.

We offer end-to-end services from system architecture and data collection to analysis, visualization, and enterprise application deployment. Our mission is to empower organizations with spatial intelligence and digital tools that drive efficiency, equity, and sustainability.

We bring together a multidisciplinary team of GIS experts, IT developers, data scientists, and field specialists to deliver:

  • Enterprise GIS and IT Solutions (Open-source & ESRI-based) .
  • Utility Mapping and Infrastructure Management.
  • Spatial Health Analytics and Disease Modeling.
  • Environmental Monitoring and Remote Sensing.
  • Field Data Collection Tools and Platforms.
  • Non-GIS System Design and Enterprise Applications.
  • Customized GIS and Data Training for Individuals and Institutions.

Our Services

Empower Your Business With Our Comprehensive Technology & IT Solutions

Utility GIS & Electricity Mapping

We build modern GIS platforms tailored to utility operations. Solutions include:

  • Asset mapping and tracing .
  • Meter integration and hourly reading updates.
  • Fault detection and ticketing.
  • Dashboards and reporting tools.

Enterprise GIS & IT Systems Development

We design and deploy powerful, scalable enterprise GIS systems and IT platforms:

  • Custom web and mobile GIS applications.
  • Integration with PostgreSQL/PostGIS, GeoServer, QGIS Server.
  • ESRI ArcGIS configuration and automation.
  • API and dashboard development.

Custom System Design (Non-GIS)

Our team of top-notch system developers also delivers high-quality, non-GIS digital systems:

  • Web and mobile enterprise solutions.
  • MIS platforms for various sectors.
  • Backend and frontend system development.
  • Secure, scalable software architecture.

Health GIS & Disease Modeling

We support ministries and partners to:

  • Map disease burdens and hotspots.
  • Integrate climate variables for risk prediction.
  • Connect GIS outputs to health information systems like DHIS2.
  • Train teams in spatial epidemiology with R and QGIS.

Environmental & Agricultural GIS

From cocoa classification to illegal mining detection:

  • Sentinel-2 and Earth Engine land cover classification.
  • Climate-smart agriculture zoning.
  • Protected area mapping and EUDR compliance.

Field Data Collection Solutions

We implement full-stack field survey solutions with:

  • ODK, KoboToolbox, Mergin Maps.
  • Offline sync capabilities.
  • Custom forms and validation.
  • Real-time data dashboards.

GIS Training & Capacity Building

Our training packages include:

  • QGIS, PostGIS, Python, R for spatial analysis.
  • Google Earth Engine for remote sensing.
  • ESRI software for enterprise users.
  • Tailored programs for individuals, teams, and institutions.
