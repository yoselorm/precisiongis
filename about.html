About Us

Empowering smart decisions through geospatial intelligence and IT innovation.

Precision GIS and Technology Consultancy Ltd is a Ghana-based company delivering innovative GIS and IT systems for real-world transformation in energy, health, agriculture, environment, and urban planning. We offer end-to-end services from system architecture and data collection to analysis, visualization, and enterprise application deployment. Our mission is to empower organizations with spatial intelligence and digital tools that drive efficiency, equity, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To design and deliver cutting-edge geospatial and IT systems that transform how organizations collect, manage, analyze, and act on data — bridging the digital divide and enabling informed, equitable, and sustainable development.

Our mission is grounded in the following core commitments:

Innovation

Harnessing open-source and proprietary technologies to develop scalable, context-aware solutions.

Capacity Building:

Empowering individuals and institutions through hands-on training, mentorship, and digital literacy.

Collaboration

Partnering with governments, private sector, academia, and civil society to co-create impactful digital ecosystems.

Excellence

Delivering world-class consultancy, system design, and training services through a team of top-tier GIS and software development professionals.

Sustainability & Equity

Prioritizing solutions that promote environmental resilience, inclusive access, and data-driven social impact.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s leading catalyst for geospatial and digital transformation, driving sustainable development through innovative, inclusive, and intelligent GIS and IT solutions.

We envision a future where every decision in health, energy, environment, and governance is guided by accurate, real-time geospatial intelligence — empowering communities, enhancing service delivery, and safeguarding resources for generations to come.
